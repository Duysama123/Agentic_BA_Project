# Sample Business Rules - E-Commerce System

## 1. Product Search Rules
- Users must be able to search products by name, category, or tags.
- The system must display search suggestions after the user types 3 characters.
- Search results must support sorting by relevance, price (low to high, high to low), and creation date.

## 2. Shopping Cart & Checkout Rules
- Guests can add items to the cart, but must log in to proceed to checkout.
- If the cart value exceeds , apply a 10% automatic discount.
- The system must check product inventory availability in real-time when the user changes item quantity in the cart.
- Order confirmation email must be sent automatically within 5 minutes of successful payment.
